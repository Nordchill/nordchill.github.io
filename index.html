About NordChill

NordChill offers carefully selected, high-quality, and affordable food and consumer goods to the Nordic market.

Enjoy a more efficient supply chain with NordChill, which shortens the path between producer and end consumer, resulting in cost savings, valuable additional sales, and increased flexibility.

In addition, NordChill acts as a communication and marketing partner, offering creative agency services, concept planning, social media management, and targeted digital advertising to support brand growth.

Discover products that are high-quality, flavorful, safe to use, and sustainably produced. Our selections are appealing to customers and offered at highly competitive prices, ensuring excellent value for your business.
