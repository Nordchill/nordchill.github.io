About us

NordChill represents carefully selected, high-quality and competitively priced food products and consumer goods in the Nordic market. We create a shorter path between producer and end consumer – resulting in reduced costs, better returns, higher product quality, and increased flexibility in the supply chain.

We are selective about the products we offer. They must be high quality, flavorful, safe to use, sustainably produced, and above all possible to sell directly at a highly competitive price.
